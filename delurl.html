Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your privacy and offer a way for users to request the deletion of their data collected through our application.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, provide your full name, the email address used with the application, and any relevant details related to your request.
  3. We will verify your identity and respond within 7 working days with confirmation of the data deletion.
